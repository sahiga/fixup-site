In a nutshell

FixUp connects you to expert plumbers, electricians, handymen, and other skilled professionals ("fixers") for instant, real-time video consultations.

The FixUp app is currently available for all Apple devices with a front-facing camera (iPhone 4+, iPod Touch 4G+, iPad 2+).

Frequently asked questions

Q: How much does FixUp cost?
A: Fixers charge anywhere between $.25 to $5 per minute. The app is free to download.

Q: Where can I use this?
A: Anywhere in the United States.

Q: How do I know that your fixers are legitimate?
A: We prescreen all fixers. Our feedback system also allows clients to rate and review fixers, providing an extra layer of screening.

Q: Do I need Wi-Fi for this?
A: No. FixUp works better on Wi-Fi, but it's not necessary.

Q: How is the video conferencing any different from FaceTime or Skype?
A: Video is just one of FixUp's many tricks. We've got a database of hundreds of service providers, an automatic billing feature using Apple Store credits, embedded GPS, a cool flashlight feature…and the list goes on.

Q: Do I have to use video? What if I just want to talk to a fixer?
A: You can turn off the video using a button on the app.

Q: What if the fixer can't help me?
A: Your fixer can easily refund either 50% or 100% of your credits.

Q: How do I pay for FixUp?
A: Apple Store credits. No credit cards necessary.

Q: I'm overwhelmed by the number of fixers you have. How can I tell which ones are closest to me, in the specialty I need?
A: FixUp automatically sorts the fixers by how many miles they are from you. If you've previously used the service and selected favorites, it lists those fixers first.

Q: Can I use FixUp with my existing [plumber/electrician/handyman/etc.]?
A: Yes, if your service provider has an Apple device. You can text the link to the app directly to your provider, or have us register your provider for you. (Email us at info@tryfixup.com.)

Q: How do I know if the fixer I choose is available at the time I need the fix?
A: FixUp displays the availability of each fixer.

Q: What if my fixer determines that my issue is beyond the scope of a video call?
A: The fixer can stop by your house or office for a full in-person consultation. The amount you paid for the video call would be deducted from the cost of the in-person consultation.

Q: I'd like to use FixUp. How do I get started?
A: Great! All you need to do is download the FixUp app onto your iPhone, iPod, or iPad and select a fixer.

Q: What about an Android version?
A: Coming soon! Email us at info@tryfixup.com to get notified once we launch for Android.
